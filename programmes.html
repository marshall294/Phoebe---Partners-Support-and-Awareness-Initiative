Upcoming Events - PROJECT PAD 4,000 GIRLS

Project Pad aims to empower 4,000 teenage girls, underprivileged women, and girls/women with disabilities by providing menstrual health management solutions, promoting inclusiveness, and breaking social stigmas surrounding menstruation. - Sponsor 1 girl: ₦2,500 - Sponsor 10 girls: ₦25,000. Your support will provide sanitary pads and menstrual health education to girls in need, promoting dignity, confidence, and educational opportunities! .

Phoebe’s & Partners Street Feeding – 1/1/2025

We started the New Year with a heartfelt mission! On January 1st, Phoebe’s Foundation provided warm meals to over 100 individuals on the streets of Suleja, offering not just nourishment but also hope to those in need. This outreach reflects our unwavering dedication to uplifting the community and making a meaningful difference in the lives of vulnerable individuals. We are deeply grateful for the opportunity to serve and remain committed to spreading love, kindness, and support to those who need it most.

Visit a Hospital Project – December 20, 2024

On December 20th, our team visited Suleja General Hospital, Niger State, bringing comfort and care to patients and their families. This heartfelt initiative touched many lives, filling the hospital with joy, hope, and a renewed sense of relief. Our goal was to offer not just support but also compassion to those facing medical challenges. Seeing the smiles, tears of gratitude, and heartfelt appreciation from patients and their loved ones was truly humbling. This experience reaffirmed the power of community outreach and the impact we can make in the lives of others. We remain dedicated to continuing such initiatives, spreading love, kindness, and unwavering support to those who need it most.

Visit to Destiny Orphanage Home – November 27, 2024

On November 27th, our team had the heartfelt opportunity to visit Destiny Orphanage Home, spending a meaningful day with the children. We arrived with gifts and essential supplies, aiming to bring joy and brighten their day. Throughout our visit, we engaged in fun activities, shared laughter, and created lasting connections with the kids. Seeing the excitement and gratitude in their eyes was truly touching. Beyond the joy, we also took the time to learn about the orphanage’s mission, the challenges they face, and the incredible work they do to provide a loving and supportive environment for these children. As we left, we were deeply inspired by the resilience and hope that radiated from the children. This visit reinforced our commitment to giving back and making a meaningful difference in our community.
